## Core Philosophy

The Civic system recognizes that members must navigate multiple social and economic contexts while maintaining their community identity and values. Technology serves to preserve connection and support individual wellbeing during necessary external engagement.

Success is measured not by isolation from existing systems but by the ability to maintain Civic principles and community connection while engaging effectively with the broader world. The goal is resilient community members who can contribute to both their Civic community and wider society while preserving their values and relationships.
